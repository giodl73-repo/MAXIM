# 03 — Respiratory System

## Lung Anatomy, Gas Exchange, Ventilation, Control

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Anatomy: conducting zone (trachea → bronchi → bronchioles) vs respiratory zone (alveoli), ~300M alveoli, type I/II pneumocytes
- Surfactant: DPPC, surface tension (LaPlace's law), why premature infants need it
- Lung volumes: TV / IRV / ERV / RV / TLC / VC / FRC — spirometry basics
- Mechanics of ventilation: diaphragm, Boyle's law, pleural pressure, compliance, airway resistance
- Gas exchange: Fick's law, diffusion capacity, V/Q matching and mismatch, dead space
- Oxygen transport: hemoglobin-oxygen dissociation curve, P50, Bohr effect, 2,3-BPG
- CO₂ transport: dissolved / bicarbonate (Henderson-Hasselbalch) / carbamino
- Acid-base: pH, PaCO₂, HCO₃⁻ — four primary disorders + compensation
- Central control: medullary respiratory centers, chemoreceptors (central CO₂ / peripheral O₂)
- Pulmonary circulation: lower pressure than systemic, hypoxic vasoconstriction
