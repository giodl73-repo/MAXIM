# 02 — Cardiovascular System

## Heart, Vasculature, Cardiac Cycle, Hemodynamics

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Heart anatomy: four chambers, four valves, coronary arteries, conduction system
- Cardiac conduction: SA node → AV node → Bundle of His → Purkinje fibers — rates and pacemaker hierarchy
- Cardiac cycle: systole/diastole, pressure-volume loop, Wiggers diagram, heart sounds S1/S2
- ECG: P/QRS/T waves — what each represents, intervals, axis deviation
- Cardiac output: CO = HR × SV; Frank-Starling law; preload/afterload/contractility
- Vascular anatomy: arteries/arterioles/capillaries/venules/veins — wall composition and function
- Hemodynamics: Poiseuille's law, resistance in series vs parallel, pulse pressure, MAP
- Microcirculation: Starling forces (hydrostatic vs oncotic), edema mechanisms
- Blood pressure regulation: baroreceptors, RAAS, ANP, sympathetic tone
- Lymphatic system: lymphatic capillaries, thoracic duct, lymph nodes
