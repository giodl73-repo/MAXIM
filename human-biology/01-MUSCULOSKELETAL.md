# 01 — Musculoskeletal System

## Bones, Joints, Muscles, Contraction Mechanism

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Skeletal system: 206 bones, axial vs appendicular, bone types (long/short/flat/irregular)
- Bone microstructure: osteons, osteoblasts/osteoclasts/osteocytes, Haversian canals
- Bone remodeling: calcium homeostasis, PTH vs calcitonin, vitamin D axis
- Joints: fibrous/cartilaginous/synovial — ROM, ligaments, cartilage
- Muscle types: skeletal (striated/voluntary), cardiac (striated/involuntary), smooth (unstriated/involuntary)
- Sliding filament mechanism: actin/myosin cross-bridge cycle, troponin/tropomyosin regulation, Ca²⁺ role
- Motor units: recruitment, summation, tetanus, fast-twitch vs slow-twitch fiber types
- Neuromuscular junction: ACh release, end-plate potential, excitation-contraction coupling
- Energy systems: ATP-PC / glycolytic / oxidative — time course and fuel sources
- Common pathologies: osteoporosis, osteoarthritis, muscular dystrophy
