# 04 — Nervous System

## CNS, PNS, ANS, Brain Regions, Reflexes

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Organization: CNS (brain + spinal cord) vs PNS (somatic + autonomic); ANS sympathetic/parasympathetic/enteric
- Brain regions: cerebral cortex lobes + primary areas (motor/sensory/visual/auditory/association), limbic system, basal ganglia, thalamus, hypothalamus, cerebellum, brainstem
- Spinal cord: gray/white matter organization, dorsal horn (sensory) vs ventral horn (motor), ascending/descending tracts
- Cranial nerves: CN I–XII — names, modalities, clinical tests
- Reflexes: monosynaptic (stretch reflex), polysynaptic (withdrawal), upper vs lower motor neuron signs
- ANS: sympathetic (T1-L2, norepinephrine/epinephrine, α/β receptors) vs parasympathetic (CN III/VII/IX/X + S2-4, ACh/muscarinic)
- Blood-brain barrier: tight junctions, astrocytic end-feet, what crosses, drug design implications
- Cerebrospinal fluid: production (choroid plexus), circulation, reabsorption, lumbar puncture
- Sensory systems: somatosensory (touch/pain/temp/proprioception), visual pathways, auditory pathways
- Neuropharmacology: receptor-drug classes, common drugs and their CNS targets
