# 05 — Endocrine System

## Hormones, Feedback Loops, Major Glands

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Hormone chemistry: peptide (insulin/GH) vs steroid (cortisol/estrogen) vs amine (thyroid/catecholamines)
- Receptor mechanisms: membrane receptors (GPCR/RTK/JAK-STAT) vs nuclear receptors (direct gene regulation)
- Hypothalamic-pituitary axis: releasing hormones → anterior pituitary trophic hormones → peripheral glands → feedback
- Anterior pituitary: GH, TSH, ACTH, FSH, LH, prolactin — releasing factors and targets
- Posterior pituitary: ADH (vasopressin) and oxytocin — release, targets, disorders
- Thyroid: T3/T4 synthesis (iodination), metabolic effects, hypo vs hyperthyroidism
- Adrenal glands: cortex (mineralocorticoids/glucocorticoids/androgens) vs medulla (catecholamines)
- Pancreas: insulin (anabolic, glucose uptake) vs glucagon (catabolic) — counterregulatory balance
- Calcium regulation: PTH / calcitonin / vitamin D triad
- Sex hormones: estrogen/progesterone cycle, testosterone, aromatase
- Growth axis: GH → IGF-1 → tissue growth; acromegaly vs dwarfism
