# Small Bodies
## Asteroids, Comets, TNOs, Kuiper Belt, Oort Cloud, Impacts

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Asteroid belt: composition gradient (C-type / S-type / M-type), Yarkovsky effect, YORP effect
- Near-Earth objects (NEOs): Amor/Apollo/Aten/Atira families; Torino scale; Planetary Defense
- Comets: nucleus structure (dirty snowball / icy dirtball), coma, ion tail vs dust tail, outgassing rates
- Short-period comets: Jupiter family (Oort reservoir scattered to disk), Halley-type
- Long-period comets: Oort Cloud origin; dynamical injection by stellar perturbations
- Trans-Neptunian Objects (TNOs): Kuiper Belt (classical/resonant/scattered), SDOs, detached objects
- Dwarf planets: Pluto (geology, N₂ ice plains, Charon tidal lock), Eris, Makemake, Quaoar
- DART mission (2022): kinetic impactor — asteroid deflection demonstrated
- Impacts and extinction: Chicxulub, K-Pg boundary, iridium anomaly, Deccan traps coincidence
- Meteorite taxonomy: chondrites (CI, CM, H, L, LL), achondrites, iron, stony-iron; parent bodies
