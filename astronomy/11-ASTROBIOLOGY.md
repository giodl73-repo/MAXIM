# Astrobiology
## Origin of Life, Habitable Environments, Biosignatures, Fermi Paradox

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Origin of life: RNA world hypothesis, hydrothermal vent (alkaline) model, Miller-Urey chemistry, LUCA
- Requirements for life (as we know it): liquid water, energy gradient, CHNOPS + metals, organic chemistry
- Extremophiles as analogs: thermophiles (Archaea), acidophiles, halophiles, psychrophiles, radioresistant (D. radiodurans)
- Solar system targets: Europa (subsurface ocean + chemistry), Enceladus (plumes, pH~9, organics detected), Mars (ancient habitability, methane controversy), Titan (hydrocarbon chemistry, possible life without water)
- Biosignatures: atmospheric disequilibrium (O₂+CH₄), surface reflectance (red edge), temporal variability, technosignatures
- JWST biosignature search: TRAPPIST-1 atmospheres; K2-18b (DMS — dimethyl sulfide — disputed)
- The Fermi Paradox: Drake equation, Great Filter, rare Earth, zoo hypothesis, dark forest
- SETI/METI history: Ozma, Arecibo message, Voyager, Breakthrough Listen
- Panspermia: lithopanspermia plausibility, directed panspermia (Crick)
