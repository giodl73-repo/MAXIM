# 06 — Organizational Change

## Kotter 8-Step, Change Resistance, Sensemaking, Organizational Learning

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Kotter's 8-step model: create urgency → coalition → vision → communicate → empower → short-term wins → consolidate gains → anchor in culture; critique (top-down, linear, change fatigue from constant urgency)
- Lewin's three-step: unfreeze (destabilize status quo) → change (movement) → refreeze (stabilize); force field analysis (driving vs restraining forces); relevance in today's continuous change environment
- Change resistance: sources (loss of control/uncertainty/competence/relationships/status), rational vs irrational resistance distinction, individual vs organizational inertia; Maurer resistance iceberg
- ADKAR (Prosci): Awareness → Desire → Knowledge → Ability → Reinforcement — individual change management focus; how to diagnose where people are stuck
- Sensemaking (Weick): how people construct meaning during ambiguous organizational events; seven properties (social/retrospective/enactive/identity-based/ongoing/focused on cues/plausibility>accuracy); lessons for change communication
- Organizational learning (Argyris/Schön): single-loop (fix errors within existing rules) vs double-loop (question the rules themselves) vs deutero-learning (learn how to learn); defensive routines that block learning
- Learning organization (Senge): five disciplines — systems thinking, personal mastery, mental models, shared vision, team learning; "The Fifth Discipline" framework; critique (aspirational, hard to operationalize)
- Culture change: Schein's change mechanisms (incrementally through primary mechanisms vs transformation), why "culture eats strategy for breakfast" is oversimplified, leadership as culture carrier
- Mergers and acquisitions: integration failure statistics (50-75% fail to deliver value), cultural integration models (assimilation/separation/integration/transformation), retention of key talent
- Agile transformation: Agile as organizational change challenge, not just development methodology; SAFe/LeSS/Spotify model critiques; pilot vs big-bang rollout
