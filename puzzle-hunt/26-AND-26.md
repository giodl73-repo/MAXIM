# 26 and 26 — The Complete Element-Compound Map

Red Joker = 26 Elements (individual puzzles)
Black Joker = 26 Compounds (synthesis puzzles combining elements)
26 + 26 = 52 = the deck

Every element appears in at least one compound. Every compound uses 2-3 elements. The two books are bonded.
