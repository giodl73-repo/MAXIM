## Review Process

1. Each reviewer reads `PUZZLE-HUNT.md` (the design document)
2. Each writes a review from their specific lens
3. Reviews saved to `reviews/`
4. Synthesis into `PUNCHLIST.md` — actionable items, scored by consensus
