## Epithet Scoring

```
★★★★★  Poetic & memorable — captures essence unexpectedly
★★★★   Clear and resonant
★★★    Generic ending or listy phrasing       ← upgrade target
★★     List of nouns, no synthesis             ← priority upgrade
```
