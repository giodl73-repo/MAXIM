## Planned Coverage

**Planned coverage:**
- Chemical thermodynamics: fugacity, activity, chemical potential, Gibbs phase rule
- Phase equilibria: VLE (Raoult's law, NRTL, UNIQUAC), LLE, solid-liquid equilibrium
- Equations of state: ideal gas, van der Waals, Peng-Robinson, Soave-Redlich-Kwong
- Material balances: steady-state and transient, recycle streams, tie components
- Energy balances: open systems, heat of reaction, adiabatic temperature
- Dimensional analysis in transport: Re, Sc, Pr, Nu, Sh, Le numbers
- Momentum transport: Navier-Stokes, viscous flow in pipes and complex geometries
- Heat transfer: conduction, convective correlations, heat exchanger design
- Mass transfer: molecular diffusion (Fick's law), convective mass transfer, mass transfer coefficients
- Simultaneous heat and mass transfer: drying, evaporation, condensation with noncondensables
- Chemical kinetics: rate laws, Arrhenius equation, reaction mechanisms
- Elementary reactions: first/second order integrated rate laws, half-life
- Complex reactions: parallel and series reactions, selectivity and yield optimization
- Catalysis: heterogeneous catalysis, adsorption (Langmuir), Eley-Rideal/Langmuir-Hinshelwood
- Reactor types: CSTR, PFR, batch, semi-batch — performance equations and design
- Non-ideal reactors: RTD (residence time distribution), dispersion model, tanks-in-series
- Distillation: McCabe-Thiele graphical method, Fenske-Underwood-Gilliland shortcut
- Absorption and stripping: packed towers, mass transfer units (NTU/HTU), flooding
- Extraction: liquid-liquid extraction, distribution coefficients, countercurrent stages
- Process control: PID controllers, control loop design, stability, cascade and feedforward
- Process safety: HAZOP, inherently safer design, relief system design (API 520/521)
