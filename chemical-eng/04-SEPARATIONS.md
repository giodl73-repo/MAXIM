# 04 — Separation Processes

## Distillation, Absorption, Extraction, Membranes, Adsorption

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Separation basis: differences in volatility, solubility, diffusivity, adsorption affinity, charge
- Phase contacting: equilibrium stages vs differential (packed) contactors
- Distillation fundamentals: relative volatility α, VLE curves, McCabe-Thiele graphical method
- Operating lines: rectifying and stripping sections, q-line (feed condition)
- Minimum reflux: Underwood equation; minimum stages: Fenske equation
- Gilliland correlation: actual stages vs N_min and R_min
- Packed column distillation: HETP vs NTU/HTU, packing types (Raschig, Pall, structured)
- Column hydraulics: flooding (vapor and liquid velocities), pressure drop correlations
- Special distillation: azeotropic (heterogeneous), extractive, pressure-swing distillation
- Absorption and stripping: gas-liquid equilibrium, absorption factor A = L/(KV), Kremser equation
- Packed tower design: NTU = ∫dy/(y*−y), HTU = G/(K_ya), overall vs individual coefficients
- Liquid-liquid extraction: distribution coefficient K_D, extraction factor E, McCabe-Thiele for LLE
- Mixer-settlers, extraction columns (RDC, Kühni), centrifugal extractors
- Solid-liquid extraction (leaching): washing stages, underflow assumptions
- Membrane separation: reverse osmosis (RO), nanofiltration, ultrafiltration, microfiltration
- Gas separation membranes: permeability, selectivity, Robeson upper bound
- Pervaporation: selective permeation of liquids, dehydration applications
- Adsorption: equilibrium isotherms (Freundlich, Langmuir, BET), fixed bed breakthrough curves
- Pressure swing adsorption (PSA): cycle design, air separation, H₂ purification
- Chromatography: plate theory (van Deemter equation), scale-up, preparative chromatography
- Crystallization: supersaturation, nucleation and growth kinetics, crystal size distribution
- Drying: psychrometrics, falling rate vs constant rate period, dryer selection
