## Coverage Notes

Oceanography covers the physical, chemical, geological, and biological study of Earth's oceans, which constitute over 70% of the planet's surface and drive global climate through heat transport, gas exchange, and the thermohaline circulation. This directory spans ocean physics (stratification, sound propagation), large-scale circulation (gyres, AMOC, upwelling), marine geology (ridges, trenches, sediment records), seawater chemistry (carbonate buffering, acidification), marine ecology (plankton to cetaceans, deep-sea ecosystems), coastal dynamics, sea-level change, and the instrumentation and observing systems used to study all of the above.
