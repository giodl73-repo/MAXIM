# 02 — Antivirals & Vaccines

## Antiviral Mechanisms, Vaccine Types, Immunization

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Antiviral drug targets: entry inhibitors, reverse transcriptase inhibitors, protease inhibitors, polymerase inhibitors, integrase inhibitors, neuraminidase inhibitors, capsid inhibitors
- HIV antiretroviral therapy: NRTI (emtricitabine/tenofovir) + INSTI (dolutegravir) standard backbone; drug classes; resistance mutations; undetectable = untransmittable (U=U)
- Influenza antivirals: oseltamivir/zanamivir (neuraminidase inhibitors), baloxavir (cap-dependent endonuclease); treatment window; resistance
- Herpesvirus antivirals: acyclovir (thymidine kinase activation → chain termination), valacyclovir (prodrug), ganciclovir (CMV), foscarnet (PPi analog, no TK needed)
- HCV direct-acting antivirals: NS3/4A protease inhibitors (-previr), NS5B polymerase inhibitors (-buvir), NS5A inhibitors (-asvir); pan-genotypic regimens; cure rates >95%
- HBV antivirals: tenofovir/entecavir (reverse transcriptase inhibitors), pegylated interferon; suppression not cure; long-term therapy
- COVID-19 antivirals: nirmatrelvir/ritonavir (3CL protease), remdesivir (RNA polymerase), molnupiravir (mutagenesis)
- Vaccine types: live attenuated (MMR/VZV/rotavirus/yellow fever), inactivated (IPV/influenza/HepA), subunit/recombinant (HepB/HPV/pertussis), conjugate (Hib/PCV/MenC), mRNA (COVID-19/influenza), viral vector (Ebola/adenovirus-based COVID)
- Adjuvants: alum, AS04 (MPL+alum), MF59 — mechanisms of immune enhancement
- Immunization schedules: ACIP childhood schedule key milestones, adult vaccines, travel vaccines
