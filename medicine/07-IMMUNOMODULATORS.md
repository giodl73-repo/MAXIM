# 07 — Immunomodulators

## Biologics, mAbs, Immunosuppressants, JAK Inhibitors

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Immunosuppression in transplant: induction (anti-thymocyte globulin/basiliximab) + maintenance triple therapy (tacrolimus/calcineurin inhibitor + mycophenolate/antimetabolite + prednisone)
- Calcineurin inhibitors: tacrolimus/cyclosporine — FKBP12-mTOR/cyclophilin-calcineurin; IL-2 transcription blockade; nephrotoxicity, drug interactions (CYP3A4)
- mTOR inhibitors: sirolimus/everolimus — FKBP12-mTOR complex; antiproliferative; dyslipidemia; lung toxicity
- Mycophenolate: IMPDH inhibitor → guanosine depletion → lymphocyte-selective antiproliferative
- TNF inhibitors: infliximab/adalimumab/etanercept — first biologics for RA/IBD/psoriasis; TB reactivation screening; anti-drug antibodies
- IL-6 inhibitors: tocilizumab/sarilumab — RA, cytokine release syndrome (COVID-19/CAR-T)
- IL-17/IL-23 inhibitors: secukinumab (IL-17A), ixekizumab, guselkumab/risankizumab (IL-23p19) — psoriasis/PsA/AS
- IL-4/13 inhibitors: dupilumab — atopic dermatitis, asthma, eosinophilic esophagitis
- B-cell depletion: rituximab (CD20) — RA, vasculitis, MS, lymphoma
- JAK inhibitors (-tinib): tofacitinib/baricitinib/upadacitinib — intracellular signaling blockade; pan-JAK vs selective; VTE/malignancy signals
- IVIG: pooled IgG from donors — used in CVID, ITP, Kawasaki, GBS, CIDP — mechanism not fully understood
- Complement inhibitors: eculizumab/ravulizumab (C5 mAb) — PNH, aHUS
