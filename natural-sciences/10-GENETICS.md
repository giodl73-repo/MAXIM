# 10 — Genetics & Molecular Biology

## DNA, Replication, Transcription, Translation, Mutations, CRISPR

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- DNA structure: antiparallel double helix, Watson-Crick base pairing (A-T 2H, G-C 3H), major/minor groove
- DNA supercoiling: B-form (10.5 bp/turn), topoisomerases I/II, gyrase
- Genome organization: chromatin, chromosome structure, centromeres, telomeres
- DNA replication: semi-conservative (Meselson-Stahl), leading vs lagging strand, Okazaki fragments
- Replication machinery: helicase, primase, DNA pol III (prokaryote) / pol δ/ε (eukaryote), ligase
- Eukaryotic origins: multiple origins of replication, licensing (MCM helicase loading), S phase timing
- Transcription in prokaryotes: RNA polymerase σ factor, -10/-35 promoter elements, open complex
- Transcription in eukaryotes: RNA pol I/II/III, general transcription factors (TFIIA/B/D/E/F/H), mediator
- mRNA processing: 5' capping (m⁷G), splicing (spliceosome, snRNPs, lariat), 3' polyadenylation
- Pre-mRNA splicing: GU-AG rule, branch point A, exon definition vs intron definition
- Alternative splicing: exon skipping, intron retention, alternative 5'/3' ss; ~95% human genes
- Translation: ribosomes (large + small subunits), tRNA aminoacylation (aminoacyl-tRNA synthetases)
- Genetic code: 64 codons, 61 coding + 3 stop, degeneracy (wobble base), near-universal
- Translation mechanism: initiation, elongation (EF-Tu, EF-G), termination (release factors)
- Mutations: point mutations (transition vs transversion), insertions, deletions, frame shifts, nonsense
- DNA repair mechanisms: mismatch repair, nucleotide excision repair, base excision repair, homologous recombination, NHEJ
- Epigenetics: DNA methylation (5-methylcytosine, CpG islands), histone modifications (acetylation/methylation), PRC2, DNMT
- Transposons: Class I (retrotransposons) and Class II (DNA transposons); ~45% of human genome
- RNA interference: siRNA vs miRNA biogenesis, RISC complex, AGO2 slicer, gene silencing
- CRISPR-Cas9: mechanism (guide RNA + Cas9 + PAM + DSB), HDR vs NHEJ repair, off-targets
- CRISPR applications: gene knockout, activation/repression (dCas9), base editing, prime editing
- Genome sequencing: Sanger (chain termination), NGS (Illumina bridge PCR + SBS), long-read (PacBio/ONT)
- Gene expression analysis: RNA-seq pipeline (alignment, quantification, DESeq2), single-cell (10x Genomics)
- Mendelian genetics: dominance, segregation, independent assortment, recombination frequency → map distance
- Linkage and mapping: LOD score, haplotype blocks, LD (r², D'), GWAS
