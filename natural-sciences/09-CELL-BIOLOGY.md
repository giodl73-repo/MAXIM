# 09 — Cell Biology

## Cell Structure, Membranes, Organelles, Signaling, Division

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Prokaryote vs eukaryote: scale, compartmentalization, endosymbiotic theory (mitochondria, chloroplasts)
- Cell sizes: bacteria (~1–10 µm), eukaryotic cells (~10–100 µm), surface-to-volume ratio constraint
- Plasma membrane: fluid mosaic model (Singer-Nicolson), phospholipid bilayer, membrane proteins
- Membrane lipids: phospholipids (PC, PE, PS), cholesterol (fluidity modulator), sphingomyelin, lipid rafts
- Membrane transport: simple diffusion, facilitated diffusion (channels, carriers), active transport (pumps)
- Na⁺/K⁺-ATPase: electrogenic pump, resting membrane potential, cardiac glycosides
- Vesicle trafficking: ER → Golgi → plasma membrane; SNARE proteins, Rab GTPases
- Endocytosis: clathrin-mediated (receptor-mediated), phagocytosis, macropinocytosis
- Exocytosis: constitutive vs regulated; neurotransmitter release
- Nucleus: nuclear envelope (double membrane + NPCs), chromatin organization (euchromatin vs heterochromatin)
- Nucleosome: histone octamer (H2A×2, H2B×2, H3×2, H4×2), 147 bp DNA, 30 nm fiber
- Mitochondria: cristae, matrix, electron transport chain (complexes I–IV), chemiosmosis
- Mitochondrial ATP synthesis: F₀F₁-ATPase, proton motive force, ~30–32 ATP per glucose
- Endoplasmic reticulum: rough ER (protein synthesis), smooth ER (lipid synthesis, Ca²⁺ storage)
- Golgi apparatus: cis/medial/trans, protein glycosylation and sorting, secretory pathway
- Lysosomes: hydrolytic enzymes, acidic pH, autophagy (mTOR pathway)
- Cytoskeleton: microfilaments (actin, 7 nm), intermediate filaments (8–10 nm), microtubules (25 nm)
- Cell motility: myosin motor proteins, kinesin/dynein on microtubules, lamellipodium formation
- Cell signaling overview: signal molecule → receptor → second messenger → effector → response
- G protein-coupled receptors (GPCRs): heterotrimeric G proteins, cAMP/PKA, PLC/IP₃/DAG/PKC
- Receptor tyrosine kinases (RTKs): autophosphorylation, Ras/MAPK cascade, PI3K/Akt
- Signal transduction cascades: amplification, crosstalk, feedback regulation
- Cell cycle: G₁, S, G₂, M phases; checkpoints (G₁/S, G₂/M, spindle assembly)
- Cyclins and CDKs: cyclin D/CDK4-6 (G₁), cyclin E/CDK2 (S), cyclin B/CDK1 (M)
- Mitosis: PMAT — prophase, metaphase, anaphase, telophase; spindle apparatus; kinetochore
- Meiosis: meiosis I (homolog separation) and II (chromatid separation); crossing over; genetic diversity
