# 07 — Organic Chemistry

## Functional Groups, Reaction Mechanisms, Stereochemistry, Synthesis

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Carbon's versatility: sp³/sp²/sp hybridization, bond angles, π bonding, conjugation
- Functional group hierarchy: alcohols, aldehydes, ketones, carboxylic acids, esters, amides, amines, ethers, thiols
- IUPAC nomenclature: naming rules for main classes, locants, stereodescriptors (R/S, E/Z)
- Stereochemistry: chirality, enantiomers, diastereomers, meso compounds
- R/S configuration: CIP rules (priority based on atomic number)
- E/Z nomenclature for double bonds; restricted rotation
- Reaction mechanisms: arrow pushing, nucleophile, electrophile, leaving group
- Substitution reactions: SN1 vs SN2 mechanisms, stereochemistry outcomes, solvent effects
- Elimination reactions: E1, E2, E1cb; Zaitsev vs Hofmann regioselectivity
- Addition reactions: electrophilic addition to alkenes (Markovnikov's rule), anti-Markovnikov (HBr + peroxide)
- Free radical reactions: chain mechanism (initiation, propagation, termination), radical stability
- Aromatic chemistry: benzene resonance, Hückel's rule (4n+2 π electrons), aromaticity
- Electrophilic aromatic substitution (EAS): nitration, Friedel-Crafts, halogenation; o/p/m directors
- Nucleophilic aromatic substitution (NAS): electron-withdrawing groups required, Meisenheimer complex
- Carbonyl chemistry: nucleophilic addition to aldehydes and ketones
- Acyl substitution: carboxylic acid derivatives reactivity order (acyl chloride > anhydride > ester > amide)
- Aldol condensation: enolate formation, β-hydroxy carbonyl, dehydration
- Diels-Alder reaction: [4+2] cycloaddition, s-cis diene requirement, endo rule, retrosynthetic analysis
- Pericyclic reactions: Woodward-Hoffmann rules, FMO theory, electrocyclic, sigmatropic
- Organometallic reactions: Grignard, organolithium, Wittig, Pd-catalyzed coupling (Suzuki, Heck)
- Retrosynthetic analysis: disconnection approach, synthons, FGI (functional group interconversion)
- Protecting groups: necessity, common protecting groups for OH, NH₂, COOH
- Spectroscopy for structure determination: IR (functional group ID), ¹H NMR (chemical shift, coupling), ¹³C NMR, MS
