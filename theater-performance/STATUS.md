## Coverage Notes

Live performance as art form, distinct from cinema-film/ (recorded) and music-theory/ (sound-only). The theoretical backbone (performance studies, dramaturgy) connects to anthropology/ (ritual, liminality), rhetoric/ (speech act theory), and linguistics/ (pragmatics). Opera bridges music-theory/ and art-history/. The performance studies file establishes the intellectual link between theatrical performance and broader social behavior — a bridge to organizational-behavior/ and political-science/ (political theater, ritual in governance).
