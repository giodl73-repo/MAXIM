# 03 — Clinical & Abnormal Psychology

## DSM-5 Categories, Dimensional vs Categorical, Evidence-Based Treatments

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Classification systems: DSM-5 (American — categorical, symptom-based, atheoretical), ICD-11 (WHO — global, more dimensional), RDoC (Research Domain Criteria — NIMH dimensional framework based on neuroscience); DSM limitations (heterogeneity within categories, high comorbidity, arbitrary thresholds)
- Mood disorders: Major Depressive Disorder (criteria: 5/9 symptoms ≥2 weeks; lifetime prevalence ~17%; neurobiological models — monoamine hypothesis too simple; HPA axis dysregulation), Bipolar I/II (mania vs hypomania distinction, cycling, lithium as gold standard), Persistent Depressive Disorder (dysthymia)
- Anxiety disorders: GAD (excessive worry, cognitive model), Social Anxiety Disorder (most common anxiety disorder, avoidance as maintainer), Panic Disorder (interoceptive fear), Specific Phobias; distinction from normative anxiety; fight-flight-freeze system
- OCD and related: OCD (obsessions + compulsions, loop model), PTSD (DSM-5 own category — intrusion/avoidance/negative alterations/hyperarousal, fear network model), complex PTSD (ICD-11)
- Psychotic disorders: Schizophrenia (positive symptoms: hallucinations/delusions/disorganized; negative: flat affect/alogia/avolition; cognitive deficits; dopamine hypothesis + glutamate hypothesis), Schizoaffective, Brief Psychotic Disorder
- Personality disorders: three clusters (A-odd/B-dramatic/C-anxious), borderline PD (identity instability/impulsivity/splitting — DBT as treatment), antisocial PD (conduct disorder → ASPD pipeline), narcissistic PD; categorical vs dimensional debate (Alternative DSM-5 Model)
- Neurodevelopmental: ADHD (inattentive/hyperactive-impulsive/combined; executive function model; validity debate; stimulant treatment evidence), Autism Spectrum (social communication/restricted interests; spectrum heterogeneity; neurodiversity vs medical model)
- Evidence-based treatments: CBT (cognitive restructuring + behavioral activation + exposure — most evidence), DBT (BPD — Linehan; mindfulness + distress tolerance + emotion regulation + interpersonal effectiveness), ACT (psychological flexibility), exposure therapy (extinction learning), meta-analytic effect sizes by disorder
- Psychopharmacology interface: see medicine/04-CNS-DRUGS; psychological vs medication vs combined; dose-response; placebo response in depression (~35%); long-term outcomes debate
