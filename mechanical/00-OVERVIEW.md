## Planned Coverage

**Planned coverage:**
- Laws of thermodynamics: energy, entropy, equilibrium, the third law
- Power cycles: Carnot, Rankine (steam), Brayton (gas turbine), Diesel, Otto, Stirling
- Refrigeration and heat pump cycles: vapor compression, COP, exergy analysis
- Fluid statics: pressure, buoyancy, manometry, hydrostatic forces on surfaces
- Fluid kinematics: streamlines, pathlines, streak lines, Reynolds Transport Theorem
- Navier-Stokes equations: Newtonian fluids, viscosity, no-slip condition
- Pipe flow: laminar (Hagen-Poiseuille), turbulent (Moody chart, Darcy-Weisbach), minor losses
- External flow: drag and lift, boundary layer, Blasius, drag coefficients table
- Compressible flow: Mach number, isentropic relations, normal shocks
- Turbomachinery: pumps, fans, compressors, turbines — velocity triangles, Euler turbomachinery equation
- Heat conduction: Fourier's law, thermal resistance networks, fins, transient conduction
- Convection: Newton's law of cooling, forced/natural convection, Nusselt correlations
- Radiation: Stefan-Boltzmann, emissivity, view factors, blackbody/grey body analysis
- Heat exchangers: LMTD and NTU-effectiveness methods, counterflow advantage
- Stress and strain: elastic modulus, Poisson's ratio, stress-strain diagrams, yield criteria
- Failure theories: von Mises, Tresca, Mohr's circle, factor of safety
- Fatigue: S-N curve, Goodman diagram, stress concentration, notch sensitivity
- Machine elements: shafts, keys, couplings, gears, bearings, springs, fasteners
- Manufacturing processes: machining (turning/milling/drilling), casting, forging, welding, AM
