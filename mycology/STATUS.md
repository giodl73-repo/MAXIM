## Coverage Notes

Fungi are neither plant nor animal — the kingdom most closely related to animals, yet fundamentally different. Bridges ecology/ (mycorrhizal networks as forest infrastructure), natural-sciences/ (biochemistry of fermentation), disease/ (fungal pathogens), agriculture/ (plant pathogens), and fermentation-spirits/ (yeast as Ascomycete). The industrial file connects to chemical-eng/ and energy-systems/. The psychedelic file bridges medicine/ (clinical neuroscience of serotonin) and neuroscience/ (5-HT receptor pharmacology). The ecology file has a systems-theory dimension that resonates with computing/ (network topology of mycorrhizal webs).
