## Planned Coverage

**Planned coverage:**
- Lift/drag theory: Bernoulli vs circulation/Kutta-Joukowski, thin airfoil theory
- Boundary layer theory: laminar/turbulent transition, separation, Reynolds number regimes
- Compressible flow: Mach number, shock waves, normal/oblique shocks, Prandtl-Glauert
- Wings in 3D: induced drag, aspect ratio, Prandtl lifting line, swept wings, delta wings
- Piston engine fundamentals: 4-stroke cycle, Otto cycle, supercharging, propeller theory
- Gas turbine family: turbojet, turbofan (bypass ratio), turboprop, turboshaft
- Brayton cycle: compressor/combustor/turbine work, specific impulse, thermal efficiency
- Rocket propulsion: specific impulse, Tsiolkovsky equation, nozzle design, staging
- 6-DOF equations of motion: body axes vs earth axes, Euler angles, quaternions
- Static and dynamic stability: longitudinal/lateral/directional, phugoid, Dutch roll
- Control surfaces: elevator, ailerons, rudder; fly-by-wire (FBW) architecture
- Aircraft structural loads: lift distribution, V-n diagram, gust loads, fatigue spectra
- Materials in aerospace: Al alloys, composites (CFRP), titanium, damage tolerance
- Certification: FAR/JAR, airworthiness, flight test regimes
- Avionics architecture: ARINC 429/664, IMA, GPS/INS integration, TCAS
