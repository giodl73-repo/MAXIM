# 02 — Propulsion

## Piston Engines, Gas Turbines, Rockets, Electric Propulsion

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Thrust equation: F = ṁ(V_exit - V_inlet) + (P_exit - P_inf)·A_exit
- Specific impulse (Isp): unified metric across propulsion types
- Piston engines: 4-stroke Otto cycle, volumetric efficiency, BMEP, supercharging/turbocharging
- Propeller theory: momentum theory (actuator disk), blade element theory, propeller efficiency
- Turbojet: Brayton cycle analysis, compressor/turbine work, afterburner, T-s diagram
- Turbofan: bypass ratio, fan pressure ratio, propulsive efficiency vs Mach number
- Turboprop and turboshaft: shaft power extraction, gearbox design
- Ramjet and scramjet: no moving parts, inlet compression, operation limits (M>2)
- Rocket propulsion: chemical rockets (LOX/RP-1, solid, hybrid), Tsiolkovsky equation
- Nozzle design: de Laval nozzle, area ratio, over/under-expansion, nozzle efficiency
- Staging: parallel vs serial staging, gravity losses, specific impulse matching
- Ion and electric propulsion: specific impulse trade-off, Hall thrusters, gridded ion engines
- Engine performance maps: compressor maps (surge line, choke), turbine maps
- Engine-airframe integration: nacelle design, thrust vectoring
- Turbine materials: single-crystal superalloys, thermal barrier coatings, cooling channels
