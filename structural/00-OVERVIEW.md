## Planned Coverage

**Planned coverage:**
- Static equilibrium: ΣF = 0, ΣM = 0, support reactions, free body diagrams
- Trusses: method of joints, method of sections, space trusses
- Frames and machines: internal forces by free body diagram cuts
- Distributed loads: equivalent resultant, centroid of common shapes
- Shear and moment diagrams: relations V = dM/dx, w = dV/dx
- Stress: normal, shear, bearing stress; stress on inclined planes
- Strain: normal, shear, volumetric; engineering vs true strain
- Elastic moduli: Young's, shear modulus G, bulk modulus K, Poisson's ν
- Beam bending: Euler-Bernoulli theory, flexure formula σ = My/I
- Beam deflection: integration method, Macaulay brackets, superposition
- Shear stress in beams: shear flow q, horizontal shear stress, web shear
- Torsion: circular shafts (τ = Tr/J), thin-walled tubes (Bredt), warping
- Combined loading: axial + bending + torsion + shear; principal stresses
- Column buckling: Euler critical load, effective length factors, slenderness ratio
- Inelastic behavior: plastic moment, plastic hinge, collapse mechanisms
- Matrix structural analysis: stiffness method (direct stiffness), truss and beam elements
- Finite Element Analysis (FEA): displacement-based, shape functions, assembly, boundary conditions
- Dynamic analysis: natural frequencies, mode shapes, response spectrum, earthquake analysis
- Reinforced concrete design: ACI 318, beam design (tension-controlled), column interaction diagrams
- Steel design: AISC LRFD/ASD, W-shape selection, compact vs non-compact sections, connections
- Geotechnical: soil classification, Mohr-Coulomb failure criterion, bearing capacity, settlement
- Seismic design: ductility, base shear, response spectrum, seismic detailing
