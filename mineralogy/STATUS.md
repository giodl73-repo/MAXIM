## Coverage Notes

The mineral kingdom — the fundamental materials of rock, soil, and Earth. Bridges geology/ (mineralogy is the foundation of petrology and geochemistry), chemistry in natural-sciences/ (crystal chemistry), and materials/ (engineering materials with mineral origins). The crystal systems file connects to mathematics/ (group theory — the 230 space groups are a classification theorem) and physics/ (solid-state physics, X-ray diffraction). The economic geology file connects to energy-systems/ and transportation/ via critical mineral supply chains for batteries and electronics.
