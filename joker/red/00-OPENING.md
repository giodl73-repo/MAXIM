# The Red Joker — The Elements

*A Guided Expedition Through the 52-Card Library*

---

Fifty-two volumes. Thirteen sections. Every field I could reach,
from the shape of a crystal to the shape of a government,
shuffled into a deck of cards and dealt across a table.

Each card has a name. The Architect. The Healer.
The Timekeeper. The Fool.

But a deck has fifty-three cards.

You are holding the fifty-third.

This book is not a volume of the encyclopedia. It is a game
played across all fifty-two volumes. Twenty-six puzzles, one for
each element I chose. Each puzzle sends you into the stacks.
You will need to read. You will need to think. You will need
a pencil.

The puzzles are numbered. Not sequentially. If the numbering
seems strange, look closer. The numbers are not arbitrary.

When you have solved the thirteen marked puzzles, turn to
the last page. I have something to tell you.
