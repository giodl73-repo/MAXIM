## Coverage Notes

The molecular and cellular basis of immune defense, distinct from disease/ (pathogen-focused) and medicine/ (drug-class focused). The vaccine and immunotherapy files have strong connections to the current era of biological medicine. Bridges to genomics/ (immune repertoire sequencing, HLA genomics), microbiology/ (pathogen-immune interaction), and natural-sciences/ (biochemistry of signaling cascades). The VDJ recombination story is a beautiful instance of combinatorics generating diversity — a connection the MIT TCS learner will appreciate immediately.
