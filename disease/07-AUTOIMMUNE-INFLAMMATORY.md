# 07 — Autoimmune & Inflammatory Disease

## RA, Lupus, MS, IBD, Psoriasis — Self-Tolerance Failure

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Mechanisms of autoimmunity: molecular mimicry, bystander activation, epitope spreading, loss of Treg function, defective apoptosis
- HLA associations: HLA-B27 (ankylosing spondylitis/ReA/psoriatic/IBD arthropathy), HLA-DR4 (RA), HLA-DR2 (SLE/MS), HLA-DQ2/8 (celiac)
- Rheumatoid arthritis: synovial pannus formation, RF/anti-CCP, joint destruction, extra-articular manifestations, TNF-α central role
- Systemic lupus erythematosus: anti-dsDNA/anti-Sm/antiphospholipid antibodies, complement activation, butterfly rash, nephritis, SLICC criteria
- Multiple sclerosis: T cell-mediated CNS demyelination, plaques in white matter, relapsing-remitting vs progressive, oligoclonal bands in CSF
- Inflammatory bowel disease: Crohn's (transmural, skip lesions, granulomas, any GI) vs UC (mucosal, continuous, colon only); TH17/IL-23 pathway
- Psoriasis: epidermal hyperproliferation driven by Th17/IL-17/IL-23 axis, skin plaques, psoriatic arthritis
- Ankylosing spondylitis: enthesitis, sacroiliitis, bamboo spine, HLA-B27, TNF/IL-17 treatment
- Celiac disease: anti-tTG/anti-endomysial antibodies, HLA-DQ2/8, villous atrophy, gluten (gliadin) trigger
- Sjögren's syndrome: anti-Ro/La, exocrine gland destruction, sicca symptoms, lymphoma risk
- Type 1 diabetes as autoimmune: anti-GAD/anti-IA2/anti-insulin, HLA-DR3/4
