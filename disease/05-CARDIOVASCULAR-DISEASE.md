# 05 — Cardiovascular Disease

## Atherosclerosis, MI, Heart Failure, Arrhythmias, Hypertension

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Atherosclerosis: endothelial dysfunction → LDL oxidation → foam cell formation → fibrous plaque → vulnerable plaque rupture
- Risk factors: Framingham risk score, modifiable (dyslipidemia/HTN/DM/smoking/obesity) vs non-modifiable
- Acute coronary syndromes: stable angina vs NSTEMI vs STEMI — pathophysiology and distinction
- Myocardial infarction: zone of necrosis/injury/ischemia on ECG, biomarkers (troponin/CK-MB), time course of damage
- Heart failure: HFrEF vs HFpEF, Frank-Starling failure, compensatory mechanisms (RAAS activation, sympathetic, ventricular remodeling), BNP
- Valvular disease: aortic stenosis (most common, calcific), aortic regurgitation, mitral stenosis (rheumatic), mitral regurgitation
- Arrhythmias: AF (most common sustained arrhythmia), VT/VF, SVT, heart blocks (1st/2nd/3rd degree), long QT
- Hypertension: primary (95%, multifactorial) vs secondary causes; end-organ damage (LVH, nephropathy, retinopathy, stroke)
- Stroke: ischemic (80% — thrombotic/embolic/lacunar) vs hemorrhagic (20%); tPA window; NIHSS
- Peripheral arterial disease: ABI, claudication, critical limb ischemia
