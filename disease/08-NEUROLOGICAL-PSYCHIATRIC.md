# 08 — Neurological & Psychiatric Disease

## Neurodegenerative, Epilepsy, Stroke, Mood, Psychosis

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Alzheimer's disease: amyloid cascade hypothesis (APP → Aβ plaques), tau hyperphosphorylation (neurofibrillary tangles), cholinergic deficit, APOE ε4 risk, ApoE/presenilin mutations in FAD
- Parkinson's disease: dopaminergic neuron loss (substantia nigra), Lewy bodies (α-synuclein), basal ganglia circuit disruption, motor (tremor/rigidity/bradykinesia/postural instability) + non-motor
- ALS: upper + lower motor neuron degeneration, SOD1/C9orf72/TDP-43 mutations, riluzole, glutamate excitotoxicity
- Huntington's: CAG repeat expansion (polyglutamine), striatal neuron death, anticipation
- Epilepsy: seizure classification (focal/generalized/unknown), GABA/glutamate imbalance, channelopathies, status epilepticus management
- Stroke (detailed): arterial territories, FAST, imaging (DWI/FLAIR), penumbra concept, mechanical thrombectomy
- Demyelinating diseases: MS pathology (see autoimmune), GBS (ascending demyelinating polyradiculopathy, anti-ganglioside antibodies)
- Headache disorders: migraine (trigeminovascular, CGRP pathway), tension-type, cluster (trigeminal autonomic), secondary causes (SAH — worst headache of life)
- Psychiatric: depression (monoamine hypothesis, HPA axis dysregulation), schizophrenia (dopamine hyperactivity in mesolimbic, hypoactivity in prefrontal), ADHD, autism spectrum
- Addiction: reward circuit (VTA-NAc pathway), ΔFosB transcription factor, neuroplasticity of addiction
