## Coverage Notes

The geometrization of calculus. Bridges topology/ and mathematics/ into the physical substrate of general relativity, Yang-Mills theory, and — importantly for ML practitioners — optimization on Riemannian manifolds (natural gradient, geodesic convexity). The Lie groups file directly bridges to robotics/ (SE(3) for rigid body motion) and physics/. The MIT TCS learner will have strong abstract algebra background; this directory shows how that algebraic structure (groups, rings) acquires geometric meaning when acted out on smooth spaces.
