## Paths

### The Substrate-to-Structure Path
[`anthropology/`](../anthropology/00-OVERVIEW.md) → [`historical-geography/`](../historical-geography/00-OVERVIEW.md) → [`economic-history/`](../economic-history/00-OVERVIEW.md) → [`political-science/`](../political-science/00-OVERVIEW.md)
*Human biology and cultural evolution establish what humans are; geography constrains and channels what polities can form; economic history tracks how surplus is extracted and distributed; political science formalizes the institutional structures that result — the materialist stack from hominid to nation-state.*

### The Intellectual Lineage
[`history-of-science/`](../history-of-science/00-OVERVIEW.md) → [`philosophy/`](../philosophy/00-OVERVIEW.md) → [`mathematics/`](../mathematics/00-OVERVIEW.md)
*Scientific practice generates epistemological puzzles that philosophy formalizes; those formalizations feed back into the foundations of mathematics — Kuhn→Lakatos→Popper all live at the philosophy-of-science intersection, and Gödel is the hinge between logic and the limits of formal systems.*

### The Meaning-Making Stack
[`anthropology/`](../anthropology/00-OVERVIEW.md) → [`mythology/`](../mythology/00-OVERVIEW.md) → [`religious-studies/`](../religious-studies/00-OVERVIEW.md)
*Ritual and symbol-use appear in the archaeological record long before writing; comparative mythology identifies cross-cultural structural patterns in that symbolic output; religious studies tracks how those patterns institutionalize into doctrine, community, and political authority.*
