# 01 — Physical Geography

## Tectonics, Landforms, Rock Cycle, Geomorphology, Soils

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Plate tectonics: divergent (mid-ocean ridges, rift valleys — East African Rift, Iceland), convergent (ocean-ocean → island arcs + trenches; ocean-continent → Andes/Cascades/Japan; continent-continent → Himalayas/Alps), transform (San Andreas, North Anatolian); driving forces (mantle convection, ridge push, slab pull)
- Rock cycle: igneous (magma cooling — intrusive/plutonic: granite; extrusive/volcanic: basalt), sedimentary (erosion → deposition → lithification: sandstone/limestone/shale), metamorphic (heat + pressure: schist/gneiss/marble); each rock type's distribution and human significance
- Geomorphology: fluvial (river erosion — V-shaped valleys, meanders, deltas; peneplains), aeolian (wind — sand dunes, loess), glacial (U-shaped valleys, moraines, erratics, fjords), karst (limestone dissolution — caves/sinkholes/cenotes), coastal (erosion/deposition — cliffs, beaches, barrier islands, coral reefs)
- Mountain building (orogeny): folded mountains (Appalachians/Alps/Himalayas), volcanic arcs, island chains (Hawaii as hotspot chain — dating reveals plate velocity)
- Soils: USDA soil taxonomy (12 orders), formation factors (CLORPT: Climate/Organisms/Relief/Parent material/Time), soil profiles (O/A/B/C horizons), soil degradation (erosion/salinization/acidification), soil as slow-renewable resource
- Volcanism: shield volcanoes (Hawaii — basalt, gentle slope), stratovolcanoes (Fuji/St. Helens — felsic, explosive), calderas (Yellowstone supervolcano), volcanic hazards (pyroclastic flow/lahar/ashfall/tsunamis from flank collapse)
- Earthquakes: seismic waves (P/S/surface waves), Richter vs moment magnitude, intensity vs magnitude, liquefaction, induced seismicity (wastewater injection), global seismicity patterns matching plate boundaries
- Drainage basins and rivers: watershed divide, stream order (Strahler), base level, graded stream, river capture, alluvial fans, flood recurrence intervals
