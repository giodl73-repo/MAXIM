## Coverage Notes

Verbal art as a system — from the oral epic to the novel to digital fiction. Distinct from linguistics/ (which covers language structure) and rhetoric/ (which covers argumentative/persuasive language). Bridges history-of-science/ and philosophy/ (literature as thought experiment), art-history/ (ekphrasis, patronage parallels), and world-languages/ (language and literary tradition overlap). The critical theory file connects directly to philosophy/ (structuralism, poststructuralism as philosophical movements) and is a bridge between the two directories.
