## Coverage Notes

The art and science of persuasion, from Aristotle's systematic taxonomy to contemporary platform dynamics. Distinct from linguistics/ (structural), literature/ (aesthetic), and logic in philosophy/ (formal). Direct applications for a VP-level reader: political-science/ (framing and persuasion in policy), organizational-behavior/ (leadership communication), and law/ (forensic rhetoric). The digital rhetoric file is the most contemporary and connects to computing/ (algorithm design shaping discourse) and information-theory/ (signal/noise in communication channels).
