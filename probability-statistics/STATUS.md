## Coverage Notes

Bridges pure mathematics (measure theory, topology) to applied statistical practice. Distinct from statistics-applied/ (which covers experimental design and A/B testing) — this directory covers the mathematical foundations. Connects to information-theory/ (entropy/KL divergence), mathematics/ (measure theory), and machine learning foundations in data-science/. The MIT TCS learner will have strong prior exposure to discrete probability and combinatorics; this directory extends that into the continuous/measure-theoretic setting and the full inferential framework used in modern ML research.
