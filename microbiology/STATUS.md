## Coverage Notes

The microbial world — organisms too small to see but constituting most of Earth's biomass and metabolic diversity. Distinct from disease/ (clinical focus) and natural-sciences/ (molecular biology). Bridges immunology/ (pathogen-immune interface), genomics/ (metagenomics), ecology/ (biogeochemical cycles), and fermentation-spirits/ (yeast and bacterial fermentation). The CRISPR file connects to genomics/07-CRISPR.md — that technology was discovered by studying bacterial immune memory, making microbiology the origin story of the genomics editing revolution.
