## Coverage Notes

Cinema is both a technology and an art form — a medium that evolved from optical toys and photochemical curiosities into a global industry generating hundreds of billions annually. This directory covers the full arc: the perceptual science underlying motion pictures (phi phenomenon, flicker fusion), the silent era and the development of narrative film grammar, the introduction of synchronized sound and color, the classical Hollywood studio system and its antitrust unraveling, major world cinema movements, the technical craft of cinematography (optics, exposure, sensors), editing theory and practice, the economics of film distribution and exhibition, and the digital revolution that transformed production, distribution, and audience discovery.
