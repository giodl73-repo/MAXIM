## Coverage Notes

Hydrology is the science of water movement, distribution, and quality across the terrestrial water cycle — from precipitation through surface runoff, infiltration, groundwater recharge, evapotranspiration, and back to the atmosphere. This directory covers the full arc: rainfall-runoff mechanics and watershed analysis, groundwater systems (Darcy's law, aquifer hydraulics), river and floodplain dynamics, lake and wetland hydrology, water treatment engineering, extreme events (floods and droughts with statistical frequency analysis), and global-scale freshwater resource challenges including transboundary governance and depletion of major aquifer systems.
