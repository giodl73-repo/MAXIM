# 03 — Privacy & Data Law

## GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, Data Breach, Cross-Border Transfers

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- GDPR architecture: six lawful bases (consent/contract/legal obligation/vital interests/public task/legitimate interests), data subject rights (access/rectification/erasure/portability/objection), controller vs processor vs sub-processor
- GDPR key concepts: purpose limitation, data minimization, storage limitation, Privacy by Design (Article 25), DPA (Data Protection Authority) enforcement, fines (4% global turnover or €20M)
- CCPA/CPRA (California): opt-out of sale/sharing, right to know/delete/correct, sensitive personal information category, CPPA enforcement
- US privacy landscape: sectoral approach vs comprehensive (no federal omnibus), HIPAA (health), FERPA (education), COPPA (children), GLBA (financial), state laws proliferating
- HIPAA: PHI definition, covered entities + business associates, minimum necessary standard, breach notification rule (60-day clock), de-identification (Safe Harbor vs Expert Determination methods)
- Data breach notification: US state-by-state patchwork (all 50 states now), GDPR 72-hour supervisory authority notice, SEC materiality disclosure (2023 rule), incident response triggers
- Cross-border data transfers: Schrems I/II (EU-US Privacy Shield invalidation), Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), UK IDTA, adequacy decisions, Binding Corporate Rules
- AI and privacy: automated decision-making restrictions (GDPR Article 22), AI Act (EU) data governance requirements, biometric data as sensitive category
- Data minimization in practice: retention schedules, right to erasure operationalization, consent management platforms (CMPs)
