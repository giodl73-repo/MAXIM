# 05 — Corporate Law

## Entity Types, Fiduciary Duties, M&A, Securities, Governance

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Entity types: sole proprietorship, partnership (GP/LP/LLP), LLC (pass-through + liability shield, operating agreement), C-corp (separate taxpayer, double taxation, VC preferred), S-corp (pass-through, restrictions), benefit corp/PBC
- Delaware dominance: why Delaware (predictable courts, flexible DGCL, Court of Chancery), incorporation ≠ operating location
- Fiduciary duties of directors: duty of care (business judgment rule — courts defer to informed decisions), duty of loyalty (no self-dealing without approval), duty of good faith; Caremark duty (oversight)
- Board structure: directors vs officers, independent vs inside directors, audit/compensation/nominating committees, staggered boards as takeover defense
- Capital structure: authorized vs issued shares, common vs preferred stock, liquidation preferences, anti-dilution provisions (broad-based weighted average vs full ratchet), drag-along/tag-along rights
- M&A mechanics: asset sale vs stock sale vs merger (tax and liability implications), representations/warranties, indemnification baskets/caps, earnouts, MAC (material adverse change) clauses
- Public company: IPO process (S-1, roadshow, underwriting, lock-up), Reg FD (no selective disclosure), insider trading rules (Rule 10b-5, Rule 10b5-1 plans), Section 16 reporting
- Securities: Section 11 (false registration statements), Section 10(b) + Rule 10b-5 (fraud), private placement exemptions (Reg D/Rule 144A), accredited investor definition
- Derivative suits and class actions: shareholder derivative (suing on behalf of company), securities class actions (PSLRA pleading standards)
