# 01 — Contract Law

## Formation, Breach, Remedies, UCC, Software Licensing

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Formation elements: offer (definite terms), acceptance (mirror image rule vs UCC "battle of the forms"), consideration (past consideration doesn't count), mutual assent
- Defenses to formation: lack of capacity, duress, undue influence, fraud/misrepresentation, mutual mistake, unconscionability
- Contract interpretation: plain meaning rule, extrinsic evidence (parol evidence rule — what it blocks), course of dealing/usage of trade, ambiguity resolution
- Conditions: condition precedent/subsequent, implied conditions (good faith), substantial performance doctrine
- Breach: material vs minor breach, anticipatory repudiation, efficient breach theory (economic perspective)
- Remedies: expectation damages (benefit of bargain), reliance damages, restitution, specific performance (rare — only for unique goods/land), liquidated damages clauses
- UCC Article 2 (sale of goods): merchant rules, implied warranties (merchantability/fitness for purpose), disclaimer requirements, perfect tender rule
- Software contracts: SaaS MSAs, EULAs (enforceability of click-wrap vs browse-wrap), limitation of liability clauses, SLAs as contractual commitments
- NDAs: one-way vs mutual, what can/cannot be protected, reasonable time limits, jurisdiction choices
- Force majeure: drafting vs interpretation, COVID-19 as test case
