# 00 — Law Overview

## Legal System Taxonomy, Legal Reasoning, Sources of Law

> **STUB** — outline only, content to be authored

**Planned coverage:**
- Common law (UK/US/Commonwealth — judge-made precedent, stare decisis) vs civil law (continental Europe — codified statutes, inquisitorial system) vs religious law (sharia/halakha/canon)
- Sources of law hierarchy: constitutional → statutory → regulatory/administrative → common law precedent → contracts
- Court hierarchy (US): district courts → circuit courts of appeal → Supreme Court; state vs federal jurisdiction; cert petitions
- Legal reasoning: IRAC structure (Issue/Rule/Application/Conclusion), distinguishing cases, ratio decidendi vs obiter dicta, statutory interpretation canons
- Burden of proof: criminal (beyond reasonable doubt) vs civil (preponderance = >50%) vs clear and convincing (~75%)
- Substantive vs procedural law; equity vs law (historical split, injunctions)
- The attorney-client privilege — what it covers, what it doesn't
- How tech law differs: software patents, open source licensing, DMCA, Section 230 (intermediary liability)
- Key regulatory bodies for tech: FTC, DOJ Antitrust, FCC, CFPB, EU Commission DG COMP, ICO (UK GDPR)
